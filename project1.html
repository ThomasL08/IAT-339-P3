Project Overview:

The project has culminated in sucessfully developing a fully functional design prototype, resulting in a visually striking and informative website for an fictional architectural museum. The website offers a unique user interface with modern web design elements, catering to the ever-evolving demands of technology and consumer expectations. It is a pivotal platform for promoting the museum's collections, facilitating communication with its audiance, and engaging with architecture enthusiasts, students, researchers, and profressionals. Project Objective / Goals

The primary objective of this academic project was to design and develop a website prototype for an architectural museum using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The projects aims to demostreate the ability to create a modern, responsive, and user-friendly website within one week, serving as a proof of concept for the teams's proficiency in web development technologies, effective collboration, and creative problem solving within a one week time frame.

We had looked at many museum websites paying attention to the layout. Interation 1

The first iteration of the design focuses on the basic fundamentals of basic functionality and aesthetics. The layout is simple and easy to navigate, with clear and concise labeling of different sections to aid navigation as seen in figure 2 and 3. A minimalist color scheme with a maximum of three colors is used, along with legible typography and high-quality images. The design is responsive and optimized for different screen sizes, with intuitive navigation and clear call-to-action buttons. Accessibility is taken into consideration, and the design provides feedback to users when they interact with the interface. Loading time is optimized for fast loading times. Overall, the first iteration of the design prioritizes usability and aesthetics, laying a strong foundation for further design improvements.

Seond Iteration

The second iteration of the design builds upon the foundation laid in the first iteration by incorporating additional features to enhance functionality and usability. A search bar and a contact form are added, making it easier for users to find content and contact the website's owner. The design and layout are refined to enhance usability, with a focus on improving the user experience. The color scheme is adjusted to be more visually appealing, and the typography is optimized for readability seen in figure 4 and 5. The navigation is made more intuitive, and the placement of call-to-action buttons is optimized to guide users towards desired actions. The design is further optimized for responsiveness and accessibility, ensuring that all users can interact with the interface effectively. Overall, the second iteration of the design incorporates additional features and refinements to improve functionality and usability, making it a more user-friendly website.
